Attractive purchasing conditions for partner dealers

Diversification and risk management are essential factors in the professional trading of used cars. Operating on a local level and the lack of a broad assortment of used cars belong to the most common challenges of the used car industry.

Only a diversified portfolio strategy enables high throughput and higher contribution margins. With AUTO1.com as a reliable sourcing channel, partner dealers can actively manage their vehicle inventory, ensuring long-term competitiveness and success in the used car business.

For the digital B2B acquisition, a cross-brand inventory of over 30,000 vehicles is available to all retailers at market-based purchasing conditions, supplemented daily by over 3,000 new additions. Registered dealers across Europe have the opportunity to purchase tested and fully documented used cars directly online from AUTO1.com - 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

Remarketing partners sell cars 100% digital, fast and easy

Our Remarketing partners can sell cars B2B digitally without any risk to a network of over 60,000 dealers across Europe via AUTO1.com Remarketing. Tailored Full-Service-Remarketing ranges from evaluation to marketing and offers dealers, leasing and rental companies, fleet operators, and manufacturers an innovative and efficient digital remarketing solution.

Together with AUTO1.com dealers implement data-driven used car management. Inappropriate cars can thus be quickly evaluated, priced, and sold Europe-wide via our website and apps with just a few clicks. Through the simple AUTO1 EVA App, all data relevant to the evaluation process can be easily collected within 15 minutes by the seller.

Auto1 Group Advertising Guidelines
1. Depositing Campaign Funds ▼
Before allocating funds to any advertising campaign, clients are advised to contact our online support team to verify and confirm the correct campaign account details. After making a deposit, clients must provide the payment receipt to our support team to ensure that their accounts and campaign budgets are updated promptly.
2. Advertising Campaigns ▼
The performance and reach of each advertising campaign may vary depending on market conditions, target audiences, and seasonal trends. Campaigns are allocated based on the available advertising budget in each client account.
3. Withdrawal of Campaign Funds ▼
Clients may request withdrawal of remaining campaign funds at any time. Standard support hours for processing fund adjustments are from 9:59 AM to 10:00 PM Eastern Time (USA).
4. Referral and Partner Program ▼
Clients and advertising partners can invite other automotive brands, dealerships, or agencies to join Auto1 Group. Referring new partners earns a 20% referral commission based on the profits generated by their initial campaigns. Partners may also use referral codes to create a network of sub-agencies, generating a downline.
5. Operating Hours ▼
Clients may manage and launch advertising campaigns during daily operating hours from 8:59 AM to 9:00 PM Eastern Time (USA).
Support ▼
For clarification or assistance regarding campaign setup, budget management, or account issues, please click "Contact Us" on the platform to reach our online support team.
